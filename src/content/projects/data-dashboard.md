## Overview

A data visualization tool that lets you upload a CSV and instantly get charts, summary statistics, and export options.

## Features

- Drag-and-drop CSV upload
- Auto-generated charts based on column types
- Filter and group by any column
- Export to PNG or PDF

## What I learned

Working with Pandas DataFrames at scale taught me how important it is to profile code before optimizing — most bottlenecks were in unexpected places.
